Plant Care Guide

Keep Your Plants Happy

Essential tips on watering, light, and maintenance to help your plants flourish.

☀️

Light

Most indoor plants thrive in bright, indirect sunlight. Avoid direct afternoon sun which can scorch leaves.

💧

Watering

Water when the top inch of soil feels dry. Overwatering is the #1 plant killer — less is more.

🌡️

Temperature

Most plants prefer 60–80°F (15–27°C). Keep away from drafts, air vents, and cold windows.

🌫️

Humidity

Tropical plants love humidity. Mist regularly or place a pebble tray with water beneath the pot.

🪱

Soil

Use well-draining potting mix. Add perlite for succulents. Repot every 1–2 years as the plant grows.

🌿

Fertilizing

Feed monthly during spring and summer with a balanced liquid fertilizer. Skip feeding in winter.
